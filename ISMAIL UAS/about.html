HIJAB HISTORY

Hijab has been known by various nations and ancient Eastern societies long ago. Forms of hijab is known by the very diverse nations. Hijab is known by different ancient Greek women with hijab worn by women of the Roman and Arab ignorance. According Eipstein hijab concept in the sense of covering the head is already known before the advent of divine religions (Judaism, Christianity and Islam). The tradition of the use of the veil, which is part of the hijab, is already known in family law Asyiria. This law stipulates that the wife, daughter, widow, when traveling to public places must wear the veil.
